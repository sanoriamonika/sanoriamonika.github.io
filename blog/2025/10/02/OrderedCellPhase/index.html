Paper Summary: Active cell divisions generate fourfold orientationally ordered phase in living tissue

October 02, 2025

After stage “gastrulation” where ecto, mesho and endoderm formation the next stage in the process of the embryonic development is organogenesis, process of the formation organs. which start from the formation of germband. At germband stage these cells elongate and then partition to form the different segments. So the authors of their work ask what physical or chemical mechanism take place which distinguish these cells in different bands.

Beach Hopper

Authors claim that it is different differential rates which leads to the different flow speeds and hence different layers. They also claim the alignment defects, which are naturally formed when a cell divide, are moved out of the layers to form perfect bands of the germband.

To answer this question they worked with ““microscope looked at the specific layer of the Parhyale Hawaiensis, also known as Beach hopper or sand flea [1]. They did an in-vitro[2] experiments with the fertilised egg of the beach hopper. why took its 3d images, projected in 2d and looked at the cell division, cell alignment changes, alignment defect prorogation and hence collectively leads to well organised defect free bands that leads to the segement formation which will later develop in to organs.
