Limitation of the Decision Making

Wonder November 10, 2024

We live in this world by navigating by making decisions on what to choose, what not to. We can debate about whether we are the one who make decision or it’s being made through us. But the truth is they are being made every moment. When we try to make a decision we make it on the basis of the information that we have or we tried to get some more information to make a better decision. But the nature of this process is such that you are able to function through that limited information that has entered into your life from the time you are in this being. And the things that we don’t know are infinite compared to what we know or what we can. Given the nature that there is a few information that we can hold, our decisions are always going to be biased and this idea that someone knows more than you. Someone can do better things than you can bring a great discomfort to the person who has lived through the marker of the perfectionist.
